Our Purpose

Paws in the Park is a community event that brings together animal lovers, families, and pets of all ages to celebrate the bond between people and their animal companions.

Hosted in partnership with animal welfare organizations, this event raises awareness about responsible pet ownership, supports rescue groups, and benefits programs that provide shelter, care, and adoption opportunities for animals in need.

It is a celebration of compassion, connection, and community where laughter, fun, and love for animals take the stage.

About Paws in the Park

Paws in the Park is more than a fun day in the park. It is a celebration of kindness toward animals and the people who love them. All proceeds and community support help fund animal welfare programs, including shelter care, pet adoption, responsible ownership education, and compassionate services for animals in need.

The event unites volunteers, rescue groups, sponsors, and animal lovers with one shared goal: to make a difference for pets and families alike.
